Order Process Guide

For Buyers
  1. Add products to your cart or use "Buy Now"
  2. Complete checkout and payment
  3. Wait for the seller to ship your order
  4. Receive your items and check them
  5. Confirm receipt in "My Orders" to complete the transaction
  6. Leave a review for the product and seller (optional)
For Sellers
  1. Post your products for sale
  2. Receive notifications when your products are sold
  3. Check "My Sales" for new orders
  4. Ship the order and provide a tracking number
  5. Wait for the buyer to confirm receipt
  6. Transaction is completed and funds are released
